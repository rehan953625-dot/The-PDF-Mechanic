Why Excel to PDF?

Sending Excel sheets can be risky—formulas can break, and formatting changes on different devices. Converting to PDF "freezes" your data.

This is perfect for sharing invoices, balance sheets, and inventory lists where you want the data to remain uneditable and professional.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it support multiple sheets?

Currently, this tool extracts the first sheet of your workbook. We are working on multi-sheet support.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your financial data is processed in your browser and never sent to any server.

Can I convert wide tables?

Yes! When saving as PDF, choose "Landscape" orientation in the print window to fit more columns.
