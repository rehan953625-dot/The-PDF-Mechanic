Why Encrypt PDF?

Digital security is crucial when sharing bank statements, legal contracts, or personal ID proofs. A password-protected PDF ensures that only the intended recipient can view the content.

The PDF Mechanic uses client-side encryption. This means your password and your file never leave your computer. It is the safest way to lock a PDF.
